Greetings! I am a Sociology graduate student currently studying at The Ohio State University. My research lies in the broader area of sociology of work, social movement, and political sociology. In one theme of my study, I examine the relationship between state response and social movements. In the other, I study the new forms of labor organizing in contemporary society. Methodologically, I try to incorporate ethnographies and intervew with computational methods.
